# AI YouTube Video Analyzer

A Streamlit application designed to automatically fetch YouTube video transcripts and apply the power of Google Gemini to create concise summaries, bulleted breakdowns, core insights, and open a conversational interface over the video content.

## Features
- **Auto-Transcript Extraction**: Extracts text dynamically from standard YouTube video URLs.
- **Deep Content Analysis**: Uses `gemini-3.1-flash-lite` to extract short summaries, structural keys, insights, and structural Q&As.
- **Interactive Q&A Chat**: Instantly query specific concepts inside the video without re-watching or reading entire text logs.
